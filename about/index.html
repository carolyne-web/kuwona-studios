with their diverse backgrounds and lived experiences, michael and carolyne kleynhans' shared passion for business, innovation, design, and technology came together to establish kuwona in 2022.

further united by their love for humanity, they aim to cultivate collaborations and true partnerships with like-minded individuals to create ventures that ultimately lead to a lasting, positive impact in the world. harnessing their expertise, michael and carolyne are dedicated to shaping businesses that resonate with the ever-evolving needs of the present and future.

with a hands-on approach, they strive for excellence, deeply involving themselves in every venture from start to finish to ensure its success.
